Salisbury Cathedral: Magna Carta APp

For a university project, we were tasked with designing and producing an iOS application for use by Salisbury Cathedral to celebrate the 800th anniversary of the signing of Magna Carta.

This was a group project in which we formed mini agencies to simulate a real working enviroment. Group members included:

  • Ashley Wilkie - Project Manager
  • Kurt Stubbings (myself) - Lead Programmer
  • Alex Sisan - Programmer
  • Kaylee Hawkins - Lead Designer
  • Daniel Burden - Designer

We created an iPad only application which allowed users to view and zoom into a high-resolution image of Magna Carta. The image was overlayed with hotspots which showed where the clauses were. Tapping an overlay would bring up the translation of that clause into English. The overlays could be filtered by specific categories.

Salisbury Cathedral chose to use our application as part of their Magna Carta exhibition.
