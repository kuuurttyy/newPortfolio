BuyBits Logo Design

BuyBits is an online shop which specialises in hybrid mounting solutions for both motorcycles and cars creating and selling holders for mobile devices such as tablets and phones. They asked me to design a new logo to fit alongside the redesign of their website as they wanted to update their look. The logo was to be used on screen and for print based media.

The letter forms were altered using Adobe Illustrator to look as though they fit together, connoting the idea of the products they sell fitting together.
