Recent Work

Aylesbury Skateboards

I am a co-manager of Aylesbury Skateboards, a local skate shop. I am in charge of web design for the e-commerce store and the Wordpress blog creating many of the graphics and layouts. I also help manage the social networking accounts for the store.

Salisbury Cathedral: Magna Carta App

A University project with a live brief working with Red Balloon and Salisbury Cathedral to create an iPad application for the 800th anniversay of the signing of the Magna Carta. I was the lead developer for this project.

Face Swapping

A University Project to create an interactive installation for the Media School foyer space. Written with Processing and the OpenCV library, I created a face swapping display which tracked and swapped faces in real time.

Recovery Road

Recovery Road was a University Project where we worked in collaboration with students from other courses, Baby Cow and Channel 4 to create a Comedy Blaps style video series. I was in charge of logo design and animation, and web design for the project.

BuyBits

I created the logo for BuyBits, an e-commerse business. The logo was used for web and print. I also do other graphic design projects for both web and print for BuyBits creating images and instructional sheets for various products.

About

My name is Kurt Stubbings and i'm a digital designer and developer.

I am currently studying Digital Media Design at Bournemouth University. I have a practical knowledge in designing and developing for the web and also have experience developing iOS applications, image manipulation and video production.

I am constantly striving to improve my skills and knowledge in all areas of the digital arts, nothing is off limits. I am particually interested in exploring new technology and software to produce interesting and exciting designs which feature fun and playful interactions.

My hobbies include skateboarding (I own a skate shop), exploring, playing games and messing with pictures in Photoshop.
