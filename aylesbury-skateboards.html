Aylesbury Skateboards

Aylesbury Skateboards is an new skate shop which opened in March of 2015 in Aylesbury, Buckinghamsire. As a co-manager, I was involved with every step of the creation of the business (logo and website creation, outfitting the shop space etc.) and currently oversee its day to day operations.

The shop has an e-commerse website built using Magento which I designed and created. We also maintain a Wordpress Blog for the store which I also designed, created and continue to maintain and write posts for.

A lot of the design work is done collaboratively using a consistent design style we created to give the brand a consistent identity. It is still in it's infantile stage and is still open to change and evolution.

As well as designing for web, I have had a hand in designing for print with flyers and stickers as well as coming up with a few t-shirt graphics.
